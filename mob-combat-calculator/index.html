Rules Notes

Mob combat can speed up large battles by replacing individual enemy attack rolls with an average number of expected hits.

A mob with fewer members than the threshold needed for one hit lands no hit that round. Rows highlight when a mob is too small to connect.

Enemy critical hits and misses do not occur when using this shortcut. As enemies dwindle, you can switch back to individual attack rolls.

For full details, see Mob Combat (p.250) and Cleaving Through Creatures (p.272) in the 2014 DMG.

Always adjust to fit your table’s tone and tier of play.
