Tiny fragile cloth, paper, rope object

A Tiny object made of cloth, paper, or rope typically has AC 11, 2 hp (1d4), and occupies a 2½ ft × 2½ ft space on the grid.

By default it has no damage threshold, though you can assign one when the object is unusually reinforced or central to the scene.

Rules Notes

Hit points for Tiny through Large objects follow the DMG object table directly.

Huge and Gargantuan objects do not have fixed hit points in the DMG. Their durability is usually handled by sections, multiple components, or narrative pacing.

The suggested damage threshold ranges here, `5–15` for Huge and `15–30` for Gargantuan, give you a quick minimum-damage guideline when you want large objects to ignore minor hits.

For full details, see Objects (p.246–247) in the 2014 DMG.

Always adjust to fit your table's tone and tier of play.
