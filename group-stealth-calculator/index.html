Rules Notes

Enemy perception DC can come from passive perception or from an active perception check result. Passive perception is often the right floor to use.

A member of either side can sometimes use the Help action, when appropriate, to justify a situational advantage. This tool folds those circumstances into the situational selectors.

Non-proficient members apply a penalty equal to half the proficiency bonus, rounded down, while heavy armor or inherent disadvantage still applies -5.

Always adjust to fit your table's tone and tier of play.
