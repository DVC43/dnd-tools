Social Interaction Outcome Calculator

Set the inputs above and click Evaluate Interaction to see the outcome.

This calculator is based on the DMG “Conversation Reaction” table (p.244–245). Select the NPC’s starting attitude and the type of request being made. After roleplaying the interaction and request, input party’s Charisma check outcome to see the likely outcome and any shift in attitude.

Typically, a creature can have their attitude improved or worsened by one step maximum (Friendly ⇄ Indifferent ⇄ Hostile) from an interaction and request.
