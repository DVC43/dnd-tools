Rules Notes

This calculator is based on the DMG's conversation reaction guidance (p. 244-245). Choose the NPC's starting attitude and the type of request being made, then enter the party's Charisma check total to see the likely outcome.

Changes in NPC attitude are always at the DM's discretion. This tool suggests worsening an attitude only when a request check fails by 5 or more.

Typically, a creature's attitude improves or worsens by at most one step: Friendly, Indifferent, or Hostile.

Always adjust to fit your table's tone and tier of play.
